About Us

Building champions, one move at a time — discover the heart of Francistown's premier chess academy.

Our History

Founded in 2016 by a group of passionate chess enthusiasts, the Francistown School of Chess started with just 8 students meeting in a small community hall. We saw how chess transformed young minds—improving concentration, academic performance, and confidence.

By 2018, we had outgrown the hall and moved to our first dedicated teaching space. In 2020, we successfully adapted to online classes during the pandemic, reaching students across Botswana. Today, we are proud to be Botswana's leading chess academy, with over 150 active students, partnerships with local schools, and a reputation for producing national junior champions. Our journey is proof that big dreams can start small.

Why We Exist

Our Mission

To empower the youth of Francistown and beyond through the game of chess. We provide accessible, high-quality coaching to students of all ages, nurturing not just strong players, but strong individuals.

Our Vision

To see chess become a cornerstone of youth development across Botswana, creating a generation of critical thinkers and problem-solvers.

Our Values

Excellence · Respect · Community · Critical Thinking
